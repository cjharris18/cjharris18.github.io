About

Hi, I’m Chris Harris, a Final Year Cyber Security student at the University of Warwick, where I am also the Social Secretary of the WMG Cyber Society.

I am also GCIH certified through my involvement in the Cyber Discovery Elite program. This meant I undertook the Sans SEC504 course, which helped further my interest.

In my free time, I enjoy participating in CTF’s as well as engaging with platforms like HackTheBox. These help me keep my interest fresh and also engage with several interesting cyber security problems, I will be posting some of these writeups on this site.

The aim of this blog is to collate my personal experiences as a Cyber Security student, as well as show off things I simply find cool and interesting.
